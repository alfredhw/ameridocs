Native Peoples: Paiute, Shoshone & Washoe

  • University of Nevada, Reno — Digital Special Collections 50 oral histories of Paiute and Shoshone peoples recorded by Margaret M. Wheat (1940s–1960s), Western Shoshone Defense Project records, and Carrie Dann oral histories.
  • Nevada Historical Society — Online Catalog Searchable catalog with Native American photographs, manuscripts, and artifacts from the Society’s 500,000+ photograph collection.

Mining: The Comstock Lode & Virginia City

  • Online Archive of California — Comstock Lode Collection (1867–1923) 898 legal documents related to Virginia City mining history, court cases, and economic records of the great silver strike.
  • UNR Nevada Bureau of Mines & Geology — The Science of the Comstock Lode Educational resource on the geology, engineering, and history of the Comstock Lode.

“Battle Born”: Statehood During the Civil War (1864)

  • Nevada State Library & Archives — Utah-Nevada Territory Materials documenting the path from territory to statehood, including the telegraphed constitution sent to Washington on October 26, 1864.
  • Nevada State Library — Digital Collections 100,000+ images and territorial/state government records (1851–present), including 1861–1864 Territorial Proceedings.

Las Vegas: Hoover Dam & the Gaming City

  • UNLV Special Collections — Digital Portal Searchable digital portal with photographs, manuscripts, maps, and gaming materials documenting Las Vegas history from its founding through the modern era.
  • UNLV Libraries — Las Vegas History: Primary Sources by Era Research guide organized by topic and era, with links to Hoover Dam collections (1,000+ photographs, construction diaries, government publications) and gaming industry materials.

Basque Immigration & Sheepherding

  • University of Nevada, Reno — Sheepherders of Northern Nevada Multimedia exhibit with oral histories and the “Basque Sheepherders of the American West” photographic essay (1985) in English, Basque, Spanish, and French.
  • UNR Libraries — Nevada Range Sheep Owners Association Collection (1942–1960) Correspondence, travel documents, and procedures for bringing sheepherders from the Basque Country to the United States.

Nuclear Testing & Downwinders

  • Nevada National Security Site — Nuclear Testing Archive 386,000+ declassified documents on Nevada testing, fallout detection, and radiation health effects, with a Public Virtual Reading Room.
  • Department of Energy — OpenNet Advanced search of declassified documents from the Nuclear Testing Archive, searchable by accession number, date, and subject.
  • University of Utah — Downwinders of Utah Archive Oral histories (2016–2017) and transcripts documenting the experiences of communities affected by nuclear fallout from Nevada testing.

Water Rights & Environmental History

  • Nevada State Library & Archives — Division of Water Resources Records Archival records (1905–1951) including correspondence, reports, field notes, and legislation on Nevada water rights.
  • UNLV Special Collections — Historic Landscape of Nevada Photographs, technical reports, maps, and documents on water resources, development, and the natural environment.

Government, Archives & Reference

  • Nevada State Library, Archives and Public Records 19,000+ cubic feet of territorial and state government records (1851–present) and 14,000+ images.
  • UNLV Special Collections & Archives Primary repository for Las Vegas, gaming, Hoover Dam, and Southern Nevada history.
  • University of Nevada, Reno — Digital Archive 57,590 items in 229 collections, strong on Northern Nevada mining, ranching, Native American history, and Basque culture.

Maps, Newspapers & General Collections

  • Chronicling America — Nevada Newspapers (Library of Congress) Digitized Nevada newspaper titles including the Territorial Enterprise and Reese River Reveille, fully searchable.
  • Digital Public Library of America — Nevada Aggregated search across Nevada cultural heritage institutions contributing to DPLA.
