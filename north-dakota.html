Native Peoples & Pre-Contact

  • North Dakota Studies — Mandan, Hidatsa, Sahnish Primary source documents, photographs, and historical information on the Three Affiliated Tribes.
  • Frances Densmore Collection (State Historical Society) Notes, music manuscripts, and wax cylinder recordings of Arikara, Hidatsa, and Mandan songs and legends.
  • History and Culture of the Mandan, Hidatsa, and Sahnish (UND) University of North Dakota digital document on tribal history, culture, and educational materials.
  • North Dakota Department of Indian Affairs — Tribal Nations History and cultural information for all North Dakota tribal nations.

Exploration & Fur Trade Era

  • Journals of the Lewis and Clark Expedition (University of Nebraska) Complete journals of the Lewis and Clark Expedition with full search functionality.
  • North Dakota Studies — Lewis and Clark in North Dakota Primary sources documenting the expedition's time among the Mandan and Hidatsa (1804–1805).
  • State Historical Society — Lewis and Clark Primary Sources Documents, maps, and journal excerpts on the North Dakota portion of the expedition.

Dakota Territory (1861–1889)

  • Dakota Territory Records (State Historical Society) Territorial government records: legislative papers, governors' records, and court documents.
  • Territorial Documents and Founding Records Primary sources documenting the creation and governance of Dakota Territory, 1861–1889.
  • Dakota Territorial Records (National Archives) Microfilmed territorial records: legislature, governor, secretary, and judiciary papers.
  • Federal Records in North Dakota (State Historical Society) Federal government records maintained in the state archives.

Statehood & Settlement (1889–1920s)

  • Political Prairie Fire Collection (State Library) Documents, images, and cartoons on the Nonpartisan League and Independent Voters Association era (1910s–1930s).
  • The Nonpartisan Leader, 1915–1921 (Library of Congress) Digitized complete run of the official Nonpartisan League newspaper published in Fargo.
  • Nonpartisan League (State Historical Society) Historical overview and documentation of the NPL movement.
  • North Dakota Atlases & Plat Books (Digital Horizons) Historic county atlases (1890–1929) showing township maps, towns, and landowners across all 53 counties.

Agriculture & Rural Life

  • Agriculture Manuscript Collections (State Historical Society) Manuscript collections and records related to North Dakota agriculture.
  • Farmers Cooperatives Subject Guide Records of the North Dakota Farmers Union, Farm Bureau, and cooperative movements.
  • Homesteading in North Dakota (National Park Service) Primary source information on the homesteading experience in the northern Plains.
  • Land Records and Homestead Information (State Historical Society) Guide to accessing homestead files and land records.

20th Century: Depression, War & Cold War

  • Cold War in North Dakota (North Dakota Studies) Primary sources on North Dakota's role in the nuclear missile defense system.
  • Ronald Reagan Minuteman Missile Site (State Historical Society) Cold War-era missile alert facility preserved as a historic site.

North Dakota Government & Law

  • fiNDhistory (State Historical Society) Searchable database of museum, archives, and library collections: government records, photographs, manuscripts, and digitized materials.
  • State Archives Online Library Catalog of archival holdings including territorial, state government, and local records.
  • Archives Holdings Guide Comprehensive guide to State Archives collections organized by type.
  • Subject Guides — Archives Topical guides to government and historical records.

Newspapers & General Collections

  • Chronicling America — North Dakota Newspapers 49 digitized North Dakota newspapers (1880s–1963) from the Library of Congress, fully searchable.
  • North Dakota Newspapers (State Historical Society) Guide to over 1.4 million pages of digitized historical newspapers.
  • Digital Horizons 45,000+ items from over 45 collections: images, documents, letters, maps, newspapers, and oral histories.
  • Chester Fritz Library Special Collections (UND) Elwyn B. Robinson Department of Special Collections with manuscripts and university historical records.
  • NDSU Archives Digital Collections Digitized university and regional historical materials at North Dakota State University.
  • North Dakota State Library Digital Collections Atlases, historical documents, photographs, and government publications.
  • Primary Sources in ND History (State Historical Society) Curated primary source document sets organized by historical topic and era.

Native Nations (Modern)

  • ND Indian Affairs Commission — Tribal Nations History and cultural information for North Dakota's five federally recognized tribes.
  • Three Affiliated Tribes — MHA Nation Official Mandan, Hidatsa, and Arikara Nation website with tribal history and culture.
  • BIA Records: North Dakota (National Archives) Bureau of Indian Affairs records relating to North Dakota tribes, agencies, and reservations.
  • Fort Berthold Agency Records (FamilySearch) Guide to Fort Berthold Agency records (1885–1951) at the National Archives.
  • Spirit Lake Reservation Records (FamilySearch) Guide to Spirit Lake / Fort Totten Agency records at the National Archives.
