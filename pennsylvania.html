Native Peoples & Pre-Contact

  • Encyclopedia of Greater Philadelphia — Lenape People Scholarly essay on Lenape history and continuing presence in the Delaware Valley region.
  • West Philadelphia Collaborative History — The Original People Digital narrative on pre-contact and colonial Lenape history in the Delaware Valley.
  • Library Company of Philadelphia — Digital Collections 45,000+ digitized images including 18th-century Philadelphia records, ephemera, and manuscripts.
  • Friends Historical Library (Swarthmore College) Quaker meeting minutes, records, and manuscripts from Pennsylvania (1681 onward) documenting Penn's “holy experiment.”
  • Pennsylvania Newspaper Archive (Penn State) Searchable digitized Pennsylvania newspapers from the early 1700s through the modern era.

Revolution & Independence (1770–1789)

  • Farrand’s Records of the Constitutional Convention (Library of Congress) Complete digitized records of the 1787 Philadelphia Constitutional Convention debates and documents.
  • National Archives — Constitution of the United States (1787) Official digitized Constitution with explanatory essays on the Philadelphia Convention.
  • HSP — Constitution on Display Handwritten drafts and primary documents tracing the evolution of the Constitution.
  • Pennsylvania Digital Archives — Revolutionary Era Revolutionary-era government records including financial accounts, military records, court records, and petitions.
  • Pennsylvania State Archives (Power Library) Searchable collection of Pennsylvania's provisional governments and Continental Congress-era records.
  • Jenkins Law Library — Pennsylvania Constitutional Documents Colonial and early constitutional documents tracing Pennsylvania legal history.

Early Republic & Antebellum

  • HSP — William Still’s Underground Railroad Journal Digitized 1852–1857 journal documenting Underground Railroad activities in Pennsylvania with personal accounts of freedom seekers.
  • HSP — Uncovering William Still’s Underground Railroad Digital history project connecting Still's manuscript and published Underground Railroad Records.
  • Pennsylvania State Archives — Slavery & Underground Railroad Resources Manuscript collections on slavery, abolition, and Underground Railroad operations across Pennsylvania.

Civil War

  • PA Digital — The Battle of Gettysburg Curated primary source set with documents, photographs, and letters related to the Gettysburg campaign.
  • DPLA — Battle of Gettysburg Aggregated primary sources from multiple institutions on Pennsylvania's role in the battle.
  • Pennsylvania State Archives — Civil War Records Muster rolls, descriptive rolls, deserter lists, orders, and correspondence of Pennsylvania regiments.
  • Gettysburg College — Civil War Collections Soldier diaries, letters, and personal papers of Pennsylvania regiment members with searchable access.

Industrial Era (1870s–1920s)

  • Digital Pitt — Henry Clay Frick Business Records Carnegie correspondence and Homestead Strike documentation including scrapbooks from Pittsburgh's steel era.
  • PA Digital — The Steel Industry and Labor Unions Primary source set combining documents, photographs, and ephemera on industrialization and labor movements.
  • Pennsylvania Coal Mining History Online Coal mine accident registers, company records, and mining operation documentation from the industrial era.
  • Penn State Special Collections — Coal and Oil Industries Thomas Richards Papers (1853–1948) with correspondence on Bethlehem Steel and labor conditions.
  • PHMC — Johnstown Flood (May 31, 1889) Official state collection on the catastrophic South Fork Dam disaster.
  • Chronicling America — Johnstown Flood Coverage Historic Pennsylvania newspapers covering the 1889 Johnstown Flood.

20th Century

  • PA Digital — WWII and the Pennsylvania Homefront Primary source set on Pennsylvania's industrial production and home front contributions to World War II.
  • State Library of Pennsylvania — Three Mile Island Collection Federal documents, transcripts, maps, and corporate records related to the 1979 TMI accident.
  • Penn State Harrisburg — Three Mile Island Archives Technical reports, government responses, and materials on TMI cleanup and aftermath.
  • Dickinson College — Three Mile Island Alert (TMIA) Collection Papers, government documents, legal documents, and activist publications related to the TMI accident.

Pennsylvania Government & Law

  • Pennsylvania Digital Archives Official gateway to digitized State Archives including government records, vital statistics, and manuscripts.
  • State Library of Pennsylvania — Digital Collections State library holdings including government documents, historical records, and public collections.
  • Pennsylvania State Archives — Map Collection Over 1,000 digitized maps organized by colony/commonwealth, counties, cities, military, and transportation.

Maps, Newspapers & General Collections

  • PA Digital Pennsylvania's DPLA service hub aggregating collections from 200+ libraries, museums, and archives.
  • Pennsylvania Newspaper Archive (Penn State) Full-text searchable digitized Pennsylvania newspapers from the 1700s to present, 100+ titles.
  • Chronicling America — Pennsylvania Newspapers 102 digitized Pennsylvania newspapers via the Library of Congress.
  • Historical Society of Pennsylvania — Digital Library 600,000+ digitized items from 21 million archival holdings spanning Pennsylvania's full history.
  • Library Company of Philadelphia — Digital Collections 200+ collections with 45,000+ digitized images including maps, prints, photographs, and manuscripts.
  • Historical Maps of Pennsylvania Over 1,000 map images covering Pennsylvania geography from 1527–2003.
  • University of Pennsylvania Kislak Center Rare books, manuscripts, archives, and special collections with materials on Pennsylvania history.
  • Library of Congress — Pennsylvania Guide Comprehensive research guide to Library of Congress and partner Pennsylvania digital resources.
