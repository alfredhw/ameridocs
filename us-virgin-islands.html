Danish West Indies: Colonial Period (1672–1917)

  • Rigsarkivet (Danish National Archives) — Danish West Indies 5 million digitized pages from the Danish colonial administration (1672–1917), covering estate records, slavery documentation, tax records, church records, court records, and colonial government documents. Includes searchable registry and topic guides.
  • Arkivalieronline — Danish West Indies Collection Searchable online database providing direct access to digitized documents from the Danish West Indies local and central administration, with step-by-step guides for researching colonial records.

Sugar Plantations & Slavery

  • Digital Library of the Caribbean — Virgin Islands Collections Caribbean cultural, historical, and research materials including documents on the sugar plantation economy, slavery, and daily life in the Danish West Indies.
  • National Archives — Records of the Government of the Virgin Islands Guide to federal records documenting U.S. Virgin Islands government operations and history following the 1917 transfer from Denmark.

Transfer to the United States (1917)

  • Library of Congress — Virgin Islands Resource Guide Digital materials including the Harris & Ewing photograph collection (1905–1945), Historic American Buildings Survey (200+ illustrations), stereographs, maps, and newspapers on the Virgin Islands before and after the 1917 transfer.

Archives & Reference

  • University of the Virgin Islands — Digital Library University Archives including historic photographs documenting student life, academic ceremonies, and significant events in Virgin Islands history.
  • University of Florida — Virgin Islands Digital Collections Digital collection of Virgin Islands historical and cultural materials in partnership with UVI.
  • Library of Congress — Virgin Islands Local History & Genealogy Digital resources for researching Virgin Islands family history and local history, with links to specialized collections.

Newspapers & General Collections

  • Digital Public Library of America — Virgin Islands Aggregated search across cultural heritage institutions contributing Virgin Islands materials to DPLA.
