Samoan Culture & Fa’a Samoa

  • American Samoa Community College — Samoan Studies Institute Active research collections including fieldwork documentation, archival materials, and media collections on Samoan culture, oral histories, language, music, dance, and the traditional governance system of fa’a Samoa.
  • Library of Congress — American Samoa Folklife American Folklife Center collections preserving traditional arts and expressive culture from American Samoa, including 1940s South Pacific expedition recordings by Bruce and Sheridan Fahnestock.

Treaty of Cession & Naval Administration

  • National Archives — Records of the Government of American Samoa Guide to federal records documenting American Samoa’s governance, including U.S. Naval Station records (1919–1951) with information on the Fita Fita Guard Unit and naval administration.
  • Library of Congress — American Samoa Resource Guide Gateway to digital collections on American Samoa including government websites, folklife collections, and legal and genealogical resources.

Archives & Reference

  • American Samoa Community College — Library Over 40,000 volumes including periodicals, databases, and audio/video materials for research on American Samoa and the Pacific.
  • Library of Congress — South Pacific Islands Web Archive Archived government documents, constitutions, legislation, census data, and development plans from Pacific island governments including American Samoa.
  • National Archives — Records of the Office of Territories Federal records documenting the administration of American Samoa and other U.S. territories.
